About Me

I'm George, a game developer based in England, UK. I soley run Avogadro Games, offering game development services for Unity, Including: programming, prop modelling (including UV mapping, texturing), setting up UI solutions, level design and much more. I have been learning and working professionally for 5 years, and I'm passionate for creating games, whether it's mobile or desktop, I have plenty of experience with both!

I hope my passion can be clear through the projects I have worked on over the years, including a full, mobile, 2D adventure game which I developed on my own with platforming, puzzles, enemies, and a unique art style - I published this game to the google play store and Itch.io, you can find the links below! Another project I have been working on, is a horror-FPS game, with first-person shooting, interaction, physics-based movement controller, enemies with complex states, and unique CRT UI. These projects along with any other project I have worked on in my personal collection, has their own videos going through different systems, showcases etc, on my YouTube page which I will link down below.
